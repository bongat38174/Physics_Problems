# ✅ **Solution 3: Conservation of E
nergy (Pendulum)**
---

### **Given:**

* Length: (L = 1.0 , \text{m})
* Initial angle: (\theta = 15^\circ)
* Gravity: (g = 9.81 , \text{m/s}^2)

---

### **Step 1: Find height difference**

The vertical drop from the starting point is:

[
h = L(1 - \cos \theta)
]

[
h = 1(1 - \cos 15^\circ)
]

[
\cos 15^\circ \approx 0.966
]

[
h = 1(1 - 0.966) = 0.034 , \text{m}
]

---

### **Step 2: Apply conservation of energy**

Potential energy at top → kinetic energy at bottom:

[
mgh = \frac{1}{2}mv^2
]

Mass cancels:

[
v = \sqrt{2gh}
]

v = \sqrt{2gh}

---

### **Step 3: Calculate velocity**

[
v = \sqrt{2(9.81)(0.034)}
]

[
v = \sqrt{0.667} \approx 0.82 , \text{m/s}
]

---

### ✅ **Final Answer:**

[
\boxed{v \approx 0.82 , \text{m/s}}
]
