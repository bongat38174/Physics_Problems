# ✅ **Solution 10: Force Field and Power**

---

## **Given:**

[
x = 5t^2 - t, \quad y = 2t^3, \quad z = -3t + 2
]
[
m = 0.5 , \text{kg}
]

---

## **1. Velocity**

Velocity is the derivative of position:

[
v = \frac{dr}{dt}
]

[
v_x = \frac{dx}{dt} = 10t - 1
]
[
v_y = \frac{dy}{dt} = 6t^2
]
[
v_z = \frac{dz}{dt} = -3
]

---

### ✅ **Velocity vector:**

[
\vec v(t) = (10t - 1,; 6t^2,; -3)
]

---

## **2. Momentum**

[
\vec p = m \vec v
]

[
\vec p(t) = 0.5(10t - 1,; 6t^2,; -3)
]

[
\vec p(t) = (5t - 0.5,; 3t^2,; -1.5)
]

---

## **3. Acceleration**

[
a = \frac{dv}{dt}
]

[
a_x = 10,\quad a_y = 12t,\quad a_z = 0
]

---

### ✅ **Acceleration vector:**

[
\vec a(t) = (10,; 12t,; 0)
]

---

## **4. Force**

Using Newton’s second law:

[
\vec F = m\vec a
]

[
\vec F(t) = 0.5(10,; 12t,; 0)
]

[
\vec F(t) = (5,; 6t,; 0)
]

---

## **5. Power**

Power is:

[
P = \vec F \cdot \vec v
]

[
P = (5,; 6t,; 0) \cdot (10t - 1,; 6t^2,; -3)
]

Dot product:

[
P = 5(10t - 1) + 6t(6t^2)
]

[
P = 50t - 5 + 36t^3
]

---

  ]

* Force:
