# Problem 1: Wave Properties

## Given
- Frequency: f = 440 Hz
- Speed of sound in air: v_air = 343 m/s
- Speed of sound in water: v_water = 1482 m/s

## Formula
v = f * λ  →  λ = v / f

## Solution
- Wavelength in air:
  λ_air = 343 / 440 ≈ 0.78 m
- Wavelength in water:
  λ_water = 1482 / 440 ≈ 3.37 m

## Answer
- Wavelength in air: 0.78 m  
- Wavelength in water: 3.37 m
