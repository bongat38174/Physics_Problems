# ✅ **Problem 4: Phase Difference**

### Given:

* Distance between points: (\Delta x = \lambda/3)

---

## 🔹 Step 1: Phase Difference Formula

[
\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda} \Delta x
]

---

## 🔹 Step 2: Substitute

[\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{\lambda} \cdot \frac{\lambda}{3}
]

[
\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{3} ,\text{radians}
]

---

## ✅ **Final Answer:**

[
\boxed{\Delta \phi = \frac{2\pi}{3} \text{ radians}}
]

---

## 💡 Key Idea:

* Phase difference proportional to distance along the wave
* Distance = fraction of wavelength → phase = same fraction of (2\pi)
