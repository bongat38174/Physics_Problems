# Problem 6: Wave Equation

## Given
y(x,t) = 0.05 sin(2πx − 50πt)  
(x and y in meters, t in seconds)

Compare with the standard wave form:  
y(x,t) = A sin(kx − ωt)

---

## a) Amplitude

A = 0.05 m

---

## b) Wavelength

k = 2π  
λ = 2π / k  

λ = 2π / 2π = 1 m

---

## c) Frequency

ω = 50π  
f = ω / (2π)  

f = 50π / (2π) = 25 Hz

---

## d) Wave Speed

v = fλ  

v = 25 × 1 = 25 m/s

---

## Final Answers

- Amplitude: 0.05 m  
- Wavelength: 1 m  
- Frequency: 25 Hz  
- Wave speed: 25 m/s  

---

## Key Idea

Compare the given equation with the standard form  
y = A sin(kx − ωt) to extract A, k, and ω.
