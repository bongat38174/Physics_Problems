# Task 09 – Momentum Comparison

## Problem Statement

Compare the momentum of:

A fly

$$
m = 2 \text{ g}
$$

velocity

$$
v=10 \text{ m/s}
$$

A tennis ball

$$
m = 60 \text{ g}
$$

velocity

$$
v=1 \text{ m/s}
$$

---

## Theory

Momentum is defined as

$$
p = mv
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

Convert masses to kilograms.

Fly

$$
m = 0.002 \text{ kg}
$$

Momentum

$$
p = 0.002 \times 10
$$

$$
p = 0.02
$$

---

Tennis ball

$$
m = 0.06
$$

Momentum

$$
p = 0.06 \times 1
$$

$$
p = 0.06
$$

---

## Final Result

The **tennis ball has greater momentum**.

---

## Interpretation

Although the fly moves faster, the tennis ball's significantly larger mass produces greater momentum.
