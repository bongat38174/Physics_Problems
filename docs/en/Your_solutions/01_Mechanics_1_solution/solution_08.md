# Task 08 – Circular Motion

## Problem Statement

Determine the centripetal acceleration of a person standing on the Earth's equator.

Earth radius

$$
R = 6.378 \times 10^6 \text{ m}
$$

Earth rotation period

$$
T = 86400 \text{ s}
$$

---

## Theory

Centripetal acceleration is

$$
a_c = \omega^2 R
$$

where

$$
\omega = \frac{2\pi}{T}
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

Angular velocity

$$
\omega =
\frac{2\pi}{86400}
$$

$$
\omega \approx 7.27\times10^{-5}
$$

Centripetal acceleration

$$
a_c = \omega^2 R
$$

$$
a_c =
(7.27\times10^{-5})^2
(6.378\times10^6)
$$

$$
a_c \approx 0.034 \text{ m/s}^2
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
a_c \approx 0.034 \text{ m/s}^2
$$
