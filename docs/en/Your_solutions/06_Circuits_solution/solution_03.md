# Task 03 – Mixed Circuit

## Problem Statement

Determine the equivalent resistance of the resistor network shown in Figure image-r1.

All resistors have:

$$
R = 5\,\Omega
$$

---

## Theory

To solve mixed resistor circuits:

- Identify series branches
- Identify parallel branches
- Simplify step by step

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Inspect the diagram

Using symmetry, combine resistors that are directly in series first.

---

### Step 2: Reduce parallel combinations

Apply:

$$
\frac{1}{R_{\text{eq}}}
=
\frac{1}{R_1}
+
\frac{1}{R_2}
$$

---

### Step 3: Continue simplification

Successive reduction leads to:

$$
R_{\text{eq}} = 10\,\Omega
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
R_{\text{eq}} = 10\,\Omega
$$

---

## Interpretation

The network behaves like a single resistor of $10\,\Omega$.

Circuit reduction requires systematic simplification.
