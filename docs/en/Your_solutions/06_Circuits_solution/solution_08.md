# Task 08 – AC Voltage Equation

## Problem Statement

Given:

$$
I(t) = 2\sin(120\pi t)
$$

Resistance:

$$
R = 50\,\Omega
$$

Determine voltage equation.

---

## Theory

Ohm’s Law:

$$
V(t) = I(t)R
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Substitute

$$
V(t) = 50(2\sin(120\pi t))
$$

---

### Step 2: Simplify

$$
V(t) = 100\sin(120\pi t)
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
V(t) = 100\sin(120\pi t)\,\mathrm{V}
$$

---

## Interpretation

Voltage and current are in phase for pure resistive AC circuits.
