# Task 01 – Series and Parallel Circuit

## Problem Statement

Three resistors are given:

$$
R_1 = 15\,\Omega
$$

$$
R_2 = 30\,\Omega
$$

$$
R_3 = 50\,\Omega
$$

Battery voltage:

$$
V = 12\,\mathrm{V}
$$

Determine:

1. Equivalent resistance in series
2. Equivalent resistance in parallel
3. Battery current in each case

---

## Theory

### Series connection

For resistors in series:

$$
R_{\text{eq}} = R_1 + R_2 + R_3
$$

---

### Parallel connection

For resistors in parallel:

$$
\frac{1}{R_{\text{eq}}} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \frac{1}{R_3}
$$

---

### Ohm’s Law

Current:

$$
I = \frac{V}{R}
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

# Series Circuit

### Step 1: Equivalent resistance

$$
R_{\text{series}} = 15 + 30 + 50
$$

---

$$
R_{\text{series}} = 95\,\Omega
$$

---

### Step 2: Current

$$
I_{\text{series}} = \frac{12}{95}
$$

---

$$
I_{\text{series}} \approx 0.126\,\mathrm{A}
$$

---

# Parallel Circuit

### Step 1: Reciprocal sum

$$
\frac{1}{R_{\text{parallel}}}
=
\frac{1}{15}
+
\frac{1}{30}
+
\frac{1}{50}
$$

---

### Step 2: Common denominator

$$
\frac{1}{R_{\text{parallel}}}
=
0.0667 + 0.0333 + 0.02
$$

---

$$
\frac{1}{R_{\text{parallel}}}
=
0.12
$$

---

### Step 3: Equivalent resistance

$$
R_{\text{parallel}} = \frac{1}{0.12}
$$

---

$$
R_{\text{parallel}} \approx 8.33\,\Omega
$$

---

### Step 4: Current

$$
I_{\text{parallel}} = \frac{12}{8.33}
$$

---

$$
I_{\text{parallel}} \approx 1.44\,\mathrm{A}
$$

---

## Final Result

### Series:

$$
R_{\text{eq}} = 95\,\Omega
$$

$$
I = 0.126\,\mathrm{A}
$$

---

### Parallel:

$$
R_{\text{eq}} = 8.33\,\Omega
$$

$$
I = 1.44\,\mathrm{A}
$$

---

## Interpretation

- Series circuits increase total resistance
- Parallel circuits reduce total resistance
- Lower resistance produces greater current

Thus, parallel circuits draw significantly more current from the battery.
