# Task 09 – Current from Charge Function

## Problem Statement

Given:

$$
Q(t)=5t^2+5
$$

Determine current at:

$$
t=3\,\mathrm{s}
$$

---

## Theory

Current:

$$
I = \frac{dQ}{dt}
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Differentiate

$$
I(t)=10t
$$

---

### Step 2: Evaluate

$$
I(3)=10(3)
$$

---

$$
I=30\,\mathrm{A}
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
I=30\,\mathrm{A}
$$

---

## Interpretation

Current is the rate of charge flow.
