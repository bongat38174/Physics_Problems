# Task 10 – Average Current

## Problem Statement

Lightning transfers:

$$
Q = 30\,\mathrm{C}
$$

Time:

$$
t = 2\,\mathrm{ms}
$$

---

## Theory

Average current:

$$
I = \frac{Q}{t}
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Convert time

$$
t = 0.002\,\mathrm{s}
$$

---

### Step 2: Calculate

$$
I = \frac{30}{0.002}
$$

---

$$
I = 15000\,\mathrm{A}
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
I = 1.5\times10^4\,\mathrm{A}
$$

---

## Interpretation

Lightning carries extremely large currents.
