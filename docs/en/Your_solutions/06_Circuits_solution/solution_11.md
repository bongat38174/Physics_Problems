# Task 11 – Power and Energy

## Problem Statement

Given:

$$
R = 100\,\Omega
$$

$$
V = 50\,\mathrm{V}
$$

Determine:

1. Power
2. Energy in 5 minutes

---

## Theory

### Power:

$$
P = \frac{V^2}{R}
$$

---

### Energy:

$$
E = Pt
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Power

$$
P = \frac{50^2}{100}
$$

---

$$
P = 25\,\mathrm{W}
$$

---

### Step 2: Time

$$
t = 300\,\mathrm{s}
$$

---

### Step 3: Energy

$$
E = 25(300)
$$

---

$$
E = 7500\,\mathrm{J}
$$

---

## Final Result

### Power:

$$
P = 25\,\mathrm{W}
$$

---

### Energy:

$$
E = 7500\,\mathrm{J}
$$

---

## Interpretation

Electrical energy converts to heat in resistors.
