# Task 08 – Electromagnetic Spectrum

## Problem Statement

Arrange the following in order of increasing wavelength:

- Infrared
- Ultraviolet
- Microwaves
- X-rays
- Radio waves
- Gamma rays

---

## Theory

Electromagnetic waves differ by wavelength and frequency.

Shorter wavelength means higher frequency and energy.

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

Increasing wavelength:

$$
\text{Gamma rays} < \text{X-rays} < \text{Ultraviolet} < \text{Infrared} < \text{Microwaves} < \text{Radio waves}
$$

---

## Final Result

1. Gamma rays  
2. X-rays  
3. Ultraviolet  
4. Infrared  
5. Microwaves  
6. Radio waves  

---

## Interpretation

- Gamma rays have highest energy
- Radio waves have longest wavelength
- Frequency decreases as wavelength increases
