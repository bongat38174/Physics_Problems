# Task 07 – Wavelength and Frequency

## Problem Statement

Human eyes are most sensitive to light of wavelength:

$$
\lambda = 550\,\mathrm{nm}
$$

Determine:

1. Visible color
2. Frequency

---

## Theory

Frequency relation:

$$
f = \frac{c}{\lambda}
$$

where:

- $c = 3\times10^8\,\mathrm{m/s}$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

### Step 1: Convert wavelength

$$
550\,\mathrm{nm} = 550\times10^{-9}\,\mathrm{m}
$$

---

### Step 2: Calculate frequency

$$
f = \frac{3\times10^8}{550\times10^{-9}}
$$

---

$$
f \approx 5.45\times10^{14}\,\mathrm{Hz}
$$

---

### Step 3: Identify color

550 nm corresponds to green light.

---

## Final Result

### Color

Green

---

### Frequency

$$
f \approx 5.45\times10^{14}\,\mathrm{Hz}
$$

---

## Interpretation

Green light lies near the center of the visible spectrum, which is why human eyes are especially sensitive to it.
