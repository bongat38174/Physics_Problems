# Task 03 – Electrostatic Equilibrium

## Problem Statement

Charges:

$q_1 = 4C$, $q_2 = 9C$, separated by 2 m.

Find the equilibrium position of $q_3 = 1C$.

---

## Theory

At equilibrium:

$$
F_1 = F_2
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
\frac{4}{x^2} = \frac{9}{(2-x)^2}
$$

Solve:

$$
2 - x = \frac{3}{2}x
$$

$$
x = 0.8 \ \mathrm{m}
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
x = 0.8 \ \mathrm{m}
$$

---

## Interpretation

Equilibrium occurs closer to the smaller charge.
