# Task 05 – Field Levitation

## Problem Statement

Find electric field needed to balance gravity on a proton.

---

## Theory

$$
qE = mg
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
E = \frac{mg}{q}
$$

$$
E = \frac{1.67\times10^{-27} \cdot 9.8}{1.6\times10^{-19}}
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
E \approx 1.02 \times 10^{-7} \ \mathrm{N/C}
$$

---

## Interpretation

A small electric field can balance gravity.
