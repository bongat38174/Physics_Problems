# Task 07 – Cyclotron Motion

## Problem Statement

Electron accelerated through 5000 V enters magnetic field.

---

## Theory

$$
qV = \frac{1}{2}mv^2
$$

$$
r = \frac{mv}{qB}
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
r \approx 0.01 \ \mathrm{m}
$$

---

## Interpretation

Magnetic field causes circular motion.
