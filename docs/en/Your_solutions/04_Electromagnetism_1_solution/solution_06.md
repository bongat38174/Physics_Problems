# Task 06 – Electric Field from Charges

## Problem Statement

Charges:

$+q$ at $(-a,0)$ and $+2q$ at $(a,0)$.

---

## Theory

Electric field:

$$
\vec{E} = k \frac{q}{r^2} \hat{r}
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

The total field is the vector sum of contributions from both charges.

---

## Special Case

For $y \gg a$:

$$
\vec{E} \approx k \frac{3q}{y^2}
$$

---

## Interpretation

At large distances, system behaves like a single charge.
