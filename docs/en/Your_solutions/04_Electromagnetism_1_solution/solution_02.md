# Task 02 – Electric Potential

## Problem Statement

Charges $+1C, -2C, +3C, -4C$ are placed at the corners of a square of side $1.0 \ \mathrm{m}$.

Find the electric potential at the center.

---

## Theory

Electric potential:

$$
V = k \sum \frac{q}{r}
$$

Distance from center to corner:

$$
r = \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

Sum of charges:

$$
1 - 2 + 3 - 4 = -2
$$

Thus:

$$
V = k \frac{-2}{r}
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
V = -\frac{2k}{r}
$$

---

## Interpretation

Electric potential depends on algebraic sum of charges.
