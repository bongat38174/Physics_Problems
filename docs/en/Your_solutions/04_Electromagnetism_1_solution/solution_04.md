# Task 04 – Force Comparison

## Problem Statement

Compare electric and gravitational forces between electron and proton.

---

## Theory

Electric force:

$$
F_e = k \frac{e^2}{r^2}
$$

Gravitational force:

$$
F_g = G \frac{m_p m_e}{r^2}
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
\frac{F_e}{F_g} \approx 10^{39}
$$

---

## Interpretation

Electric force is vastly stronger than gravity.
