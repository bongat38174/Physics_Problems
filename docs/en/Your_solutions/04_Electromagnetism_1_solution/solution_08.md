# Task 08 – Lorentz Force

## Problem Statement

Find force on moving charge.

---

## Theory

$$
F = qvB
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

$$
F = (2\times10^{-19})(10^6)(0.5)
$$

---

## Final Result

$$
F = 1\times10^{-13} \ \mathrm{N}
$$

---

## Interpretation

Force acts perpendicular to motion.
