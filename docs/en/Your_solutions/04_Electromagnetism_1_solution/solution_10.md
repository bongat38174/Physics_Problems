# Task 10 – Force on Wire

## Problem Statement

Wire in magnetic field.

---

## Theory

$$
F = BIL \sin\theta
$$

---

## Step-by-Step Solution

### a) $\theta = 90^\circ$

$$
F = 10 \ \mathrm{N}
$$

### b) $\theta = 45^\circ$

$$
F \approx 7.07 \ \mathrm{N}
$$

### c) $\theta = 0^\circ$

$$
F = 0
$$

---

## Interpretation

Force depends on angle between wire and field.
